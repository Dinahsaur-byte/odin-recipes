Noodles

Description

This is a very simple recipe for homemade noodles! It's never necessary to make your own, but it can be a nice added touch to a special meal.

With a tiny number of ingredients and little work, you too can say that you created your own pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • A large pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Roll thin using flour
  3. Cut into 1/4 inch thick strips
  4. Allow to dry
  5. Boil in chicken broth for 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
